About Me

I am a PhD candidate under the supervision of Prof. Bart De Schutter, Prof. Ton van den Boom, and Dr. Shengling Shi at the Delft Center for Systems and Control, Delft University of Technology, the Netherlands. I received the B.S. degree in the School of Mechanical Engineering and Automation, Beihang University (BUAA), in 2018, and the M.S. degree (with outstanding graduation thesis and Chinese national scholarship) in the Department of Flight Dynamics and Control, Beihang University, in 2021. I was a research assistant in the group of Dr. Zhang Chen, in the Department of Automation, Tsinghua University, China, in 2021.

My PhD research topic is about learning-based control for piecewise affine systems with constraints. I am in the ERC advanced project “A novel control paradigm for large-scale hybrid networks” (CLariNet).

Research Interests
